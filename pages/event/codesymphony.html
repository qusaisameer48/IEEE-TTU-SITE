About the Event

The competition took place on 12/11/2025 at Al-Hussein Bin Talal University, organized jointly by three universities: Al-Tafila Technical University, Al-Hussein Bin Talal University, and Aqaba University of Technology. Various teams competed in a series of challenges and questions testing both their theoretical and practical IT knowledge. In addition to the competition, the event featured booths from various companies and IEEE communities, providing students with valuable insights into the latest technologies, educational opportunities, and professional development. Attendees were able to explore practical applications of IT concepts and gain direct experience from experts, adding significant educational value to the event. The competition atmosphere was filled with excitement and enthusiasm, allowing students to exchange ideas and experiences while fostering collaboration among the participating universities, making it an enjoyable and educational experience for all.
