About Us

IEEE CS (IEEE Computer Society) is one of the technical societies under the global IEEE organization. It focuses on computer science and modern computing technologies, aiming to support students of Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and related technical disciplines through technical activities, training workshops, and competitions that enhance both academic and practical skills.
