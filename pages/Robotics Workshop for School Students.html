About the Event

The IEEE Tafila Technical University Student Branch continues to demonstrate its leadership in promoting innovation and technical excellence through the organization of UNI Agents Hackathon, one of the largest Arab competitions dedicated to the development of AI Agents. The hackathon was held in collaboration with Qafza and brought together more than 300 participants from 24 Arab countries, including 281 competitors from Jordan, within a highly competitive environment aimed at delivering intelligent solutions that serve students and the academic community. The event was conducted across multiple phases, including preparation, training, and project development. Prior to project submission, participants attended free, specialized technical workshops delivered by engineer Mohammad Huria, which supported the practical application of artificial intelligence concepts. The hackathon concluded with a closing ceremony hosted at Amman Arab University, featuring official remarks, presentations of the winning projects, and an awards ceremony honoring outstanding teams.
