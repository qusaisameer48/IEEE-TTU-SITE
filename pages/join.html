Frequently Asked Questions

What is IEEE?

+

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

What benefits will I receive as a member?

+

As an IEEE TTU member, you'll gain opportunities to develop your technical and leadership skills, participate in local and international workshops and competitions, network with industry professionals, access IEEE educational resources, and build a strong resume that distinguishes you in the job market.

Is there a membership fee?

+

Yes, there is a nominal annual membership fee that helps cover the costs of events and activities. You can get details about the exact fees when you contact us. We also offer flexible payment options for students.

How can I participate in activities?

+

After registration, you'll be added to our communication channels where we announce all events and activities. You can choose to participate in activities that interest you, whether they're technical workshops, competitions, or social events.

Can I join at any time during the year?

+

Yes, we accept membership applications throughout the academic year. However, we recommend registering at the beginning of the semester to take full advantage of all planned activities and events.

What committees are available to join?

+

We have several specialized committees: Technical Committee, Public Relations Committee, Media & Design Committee, Events & Organization Committee, and Academic Committee. You can choose the committee that suits your interests and skills, or participate in more than one committee.
